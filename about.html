About me

I am a economist currently living and working in a bank in Schwerin, Germany. Many years of work in banking have taught me what it is like to work according to rules and procedures, but at the same time it has increased my need for more creative work that will encourage me to upgrade every day.

As a UX/UI Designer I like to bring the best of my ideas into the design I am creating. I am passionate about helping my clients bringing their brands to next level and telling their stories from a visual point of view.

Teamwork and collaboration are essential points while creating solutions. I love challenges and I easily adapt to the environment in which I work.
