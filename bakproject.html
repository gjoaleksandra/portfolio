DESCRIPTION
Creating a website for a startup company
The main goal of this project was to create a
website with online shop that will be offered to the consumers
as well as easy to be updated
Creating something new, in two days was very hard but also satisfying because at the end of the day we were those who did something for our client.


I was a part of a team of UX/UI designers, marketers and graphic designers. My role as UX/UI designer was to make the research and to find out what the clients want, so we, together as a team, can create the perfect solution
We started the project with design thinking so we can develop new ideas for the website and finding out what are the business and marketing goals. My working process starts with research and finding out features that were important to the startup company.
I have created competitive analysis, user flow and then I started with the wireframes.
In this part I have decided how many screens the website should have and what content it will contain.
After drawing the wireframes I have started with the UI part. The team and I chose the primary colors which we thought would best suit the business itself. We choosed the blue color because is a color that symbolizes loyalty, trust and is the color of the sky and the sea.
The whole project was another new experience for me, because it was not just a normal project but it was the biggest hackathon organized by Brainster on which I had the honor to work with colleagues from other academies.
During the hackathon I felt excitement, but also great fear that everything will not end on time. But we still succeeded. We created a website that we presented to the client. The team and I were proud of ourselves and happy to have the opportunity to work on such a project.
