SUGARWISH
Sugarwish is a unique gifting company, online gift
delivery platform on a misson to share happiness to its customers.
PROJECT
SUGARWISH
EXECUTOR
BRAINSTER
TIME
November 2021 - December 2021
DESCRIPTION
Re-designing of an e-shop
The main goal of this project is to (re)define the Send
a Sugarwish flow for the customers, flow where the user
goes through all the steps, from selecting the e-card,
choosing the type and size of the product, giving
details to the recipient, so that he can place his order.
We thought that the process we were going to do would be easy and fast, because it was working on a small part of a large website, a flow which was supposed to fit the whole website. From Sugarwish itself we got their design system from where we could exhaust a lot of components.

The first two weeks passed very quickly, and we only had research. So we realized that the time has begun to press us. From the second week we worked as a team with daily consultations, we succeeded to make a desktop and mobile version of the ordering process.
We started the research with a competitor analysis. Some competitors were given to us by the client and we have selected 3 other competitors we wanted to compare in order to make the product better.
The team and I put together survey questions that we then asked online to be answered by American users. We had 27 responses from our survey.
My part in this project was to help create the competitve analysis, the survey questions, one of the three personas, the user flow as well as the wireframes.
We draw the wireframes individually and then, after presenting them, together as a team we have created our solution for the ordering process of Sugarwish. Before we started with UI, we decided that the colors we would use would be the colors of the sugarwish logo itself. We used the colors and the font as they were on the page but we changed the buttons.
Sugarwish is a project we thought would be completed in one day, because it was working on a small part of a large website.
From the project itself I gained a lot of knowledge and above all experience. I learned that it is necessary to research well and get to know the client, his users and to appreciate the given time to work on the project.
